1/ Base raw
atk weapon / weapon radius

ex: base raw = 1008 / 4.8(GS) = 210 ATK

2/ Bonus atk in percent (%)
every bonus are multiply
ex: bonus 1 : 30%
    bonus 2 : 20%

1.3(30%) x 1.2(20%) = 1.56 (56%)

210 x 1.56 = 327.6 (~327 ATK)

3/ Bonus atk fix
every bonus are add
ex: bonus 3 : +15
327 + 15 = 342 ATK

4/ Affinity
probability of making critical hits
bonus for critical hit = 0.25(25%)
% of luck to make a critical hit : 0-100%
ex: for 80% of luck to make a crtical hit
1 + 0.25*80/100 = 1 + 0.2 = 1.2
342 x 1.2 = 410 ATK

5/ Sharp
it's a multiplicator of dmg
ex: blue Sharp
410 x 1.2 = 492 ATK
